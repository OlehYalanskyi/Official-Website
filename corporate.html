CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

Venturastic recognizes the importance of good corporate governance for a business, especially for a company that is looking to compete in the global marketplace today.
Corporate governance will be applied across all business units of the group, our processes are regularly reviewed to ensure they continue to reflect local and international developments, ensuring we are always at the forefront of excellence and the highest ethical standards.
Moral code
Venturastic always maintains high standards of corporate conduct towards its employees, clients, governments and the wider society in which we operate.
The purpose of our Code of Ethics is to ensure that the board of directors, executive advisers and employees across the group clearly understand the principles that are important to the conduct of our business.
These principles and values are essential to maintaining and enhancing Venturastic's image as a responsible and trusted business partner, employer and corporate citizen.

Finance and Investment Committee

Venturastic management will appoint internal advisors as well as establish an independent external finance and investment committee to assess and recommend potential investments, asset divestitures, mergers and acquisitions, restructurings, spin-offs, joint ventures, partnerships, corporate actions (e.g. share dividends, etc.), capital raising (e.g. public offerings, bond issuance, bank loans, etc.), revaluation assets, wealth management and other corporate financial activities for the Group. This committee will also oversee existing investments and oversee their management to meet targets. efficiency.

Audit and Risk Committee

The primary function of the Audit and Risk Management Committee is to assist the board of Venturastic in the performance of its oversight functions. He will review the system of internal control and risk management, the process of corporate governance, the financial reporting process, the audit process and the Group's process for monitoring compliance with laws and regulations and its Code of Ethics. In carrying out its duties, the Audit and Risk Management Committee will support effective working relationships with Venturastic's Board of Directors, business units and management, and internal and external auditors.

Ethics committee

The Ethics Committee is established to assist the Venturastic Board in fulfilling its responsibilities related to (a) complying with all legal requirements to which it is subject, including all applicable rules and laws (b) developing, implementation and implementation of the Compliance and Ethics Program; and (c) matters relating to the Code of Ethics. The ethics committee helps the board set the right tone at the top and also promotes a strong culture of compliance.
