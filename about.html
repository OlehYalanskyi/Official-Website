About Us

Venturastic Fund is a special purpose holding company (SPIC) created for the purpose of starting and building a business, as well as mergers and acquisitions.

It will aim to become a strong global player by building a proven track record and making significant strides in the next 5 years.

We will build and target businesses with stealth revenue growth potential, sound intellectual property, and strong market positions in their respective industries. By investing in a large-cap business, opportunities which will generate predictable cash flows and an attractive value proposition, we also want to contribute more to economic growth in the countries where we expand our presence, while promoting sustainable methods of doing business, using the opportunities of many to benefit both mature and developing businesses.

Our goal will be to create cash capital that will serve as a solid foundation for growth, facilitating expansion through business partnerships, in addition to this, Venturastic will also maintain close relationships with a number of local and foreign financial institutions

Driven by a philosophy focused on delivering value to stakeholders, Venturastic will successfully engage in several large business ventures and business portfolios ranging from information, communications and technology, broadcast media, fashion and luxury goods that will be the backbone of its economy.

Philosophy

Our investment philosophy relies heavily on value creation. At Venturastic, we create success stories. We don't just invest, we fully participate in them, and this is what will allow us to stand out from the rest.

Our Faith

We founded Venturastic out of the simple belief that the most valuable companies of our time will be those that value their people. We regard profit and motive as a virtuous circle, and in the right combination they form a cogwheel that will bring huge financial returns by changing capitalism for the better.

More than investment, leverage comes from experience, knowledge, and mentoring, and it takes some enjoyment to challenge the status quo and bring about change.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We sponsor patient capital and strive to support leaders from marginalized communities who demonstrate the determination, skills, imagination and understanding of what it takes to create change. Our program is designed to connect and develop a range of entrepreneurs, both micro and social, who are committed to fighting poverty and injustice in their communities.

Patient Capital is not a grant, it is an investment designed to recoup its principal. He will not seek to maximize the financial return on his investment as we seek to maximize the social impact, capitalize on the creation of markets to fight poverty, and enable growth for both micro and social enterprises.

With our patient capital program, we as an investor are willing to make a financial investment in a business without the expectation of making quick profits, instead investors will require reporting in the form of capital returns and substantiated evidence that the company can grow sustainably. in the long term. If we do have a very large success story to share, we as a team will be proud to be a part of that success.

Every year we aim to invest in 2 projects as a patient investor and support these projects with our experience, business advice and strategic support to ensure their success and become self-sustaining.

We do not just provide capital, but we support projects with our knowledge and experience.
  • Leveraging investment opportunities to make a difference for people living in poverty
  • Using patient capital to close the funding gap
  • Capital structuring for innovation
  • Attracting Angel Investment to the Poor
