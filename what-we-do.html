WHAT ARE WE DOING

We create a business that will use breakthrough thinking, rethinking how to build a profitable enterprise.

We will define, conceive and build a business that will create significant long-term value for our investment community: creating business associations in the industry that will complement the experience and knowledge of our management team, Board of Directors, consultants and external operating partners.

We would also be willing to accept a high degree of capital, legal and situational complexity in a business combination if we believe that it could bring rewards that justify such additional complexity, especially if such issues can be resolved in connection with and as a result of a combination of internal experience.

SPIV - way of the Venturastic

We create our own private network structure of special investment vehicles (SPIV) for the projects we launch, which will allow us to be truthful about the vision and the project created.

We do not charge investment management fees or burden the project with high underwriting fees and other fund management costs. We bring the project to investors directly, through a network of partners and brokerage firms.

We will add value by growing the company and expanding it into a form that will increase its valuation, thereby delivering a higher ROI after the first IPO listing. All projects will be double listed on the largest stock exchanges.

When you invest in one of our projects, you not only receive the dedicated resources of some of the most diverse minds in the industry, but you also become part of the venture capital community and will always be the first to invest in all subsequent projects. projects to be provided at preferential rates.

We don't just build businesses, we build partnerships, build a community of investors, break down barriers and unleash the power of global collaboration.

Community of investors without borders.

We at Venturastic aim to invest in very narrow areas. Committed to stimulating economic growth and making a positive impact.

We will do this by uniting and creating a new kind of business angels without borders.

As industries change, so do we. As technology advances, we evolve with it. And because we are all entrepreneurs ourselves, founders and investors who grew out of a start-up and small and medium-sized businesses. We want to change the way we do investment, break down barriers and boundaries, allowing a new wave of angel investing.

Restructuring methodology

Our industry relationships and leadership enable us to diligently apply the lens of our focused strategy to ensure we only spend time on the most promising investments, allowing us to focus and leave no stone unturned.

We are not only proud of investments, we are proud of our business process reengineering expertise, allowing us to cross-reference and compare investment opportunities in a way that we believe few people in our industry can.

We study competitors, suppliers, customers and members of our network of professionals to create a holistic picture that will allow us to ultimately invest in only those that we believe represent the best opportunities.

Principles of interaction

Update capital
– allocate the necessary financial resources to the business to achieve operational efficiency and recalibrate the capital structure.
Restructure
– review the debt structure through restructuring and/or refinancing initiatives.
Reorganize
– re-evaluate and re-engineer the operating and business models of companies, as well as revise the management structure and governance.
Restart
– supporting the development and growth of invested companies, including the injection of additional resources to accelerate growth, including through additional acquisitions.
Deal type
– Buyout (controlling stake) to support generational transition, expansion to support development programs.

Research based investment methodology

Our investment analysts will conduct various face-to-face meetings with companies visiting factories, facilities and headquarters as this helps to gain a deep understanding of the operations and in this way they do more than just give recommendations.

Our in-depth and fundamental research is the backbone of and is critical to the results we achieve for our investment community.

From the lounge to the boardroom, we get to know them

We will create portfolios with meticulous attention to detail, developing a deep understanding and carefully selecting the investments we believe will best fit our experience and expertise. We believe that good investments are built on close partnerships, strong alignment and deep personal relationships.

Our research is more than just numbers. We don't just stop at tables and site visits, we get to know the people who make the company work.

We believe that the best way to get to know a company is to get to know the people behind the business, which is why we want to know both senior management and employees in the field.

Special purpose investment vehicle (SPIV) model
